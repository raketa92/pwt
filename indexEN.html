About us

The "PWT industrial and water technology group" company has great potential and technical base in the construction of industrial facilities for the processing and reuse of domestic and industrial wastewater, desalination plants and other similar facilities. We offer services in all areas: from laboratory experiments, research, pilot models, process design, project management, training, manufacturing and equipment supply, to turnkey facility construction.

The "PWT industrial and water technology group" jointly with its business partners is one of the leading general contractors for the planning, implementation and operation of turnkey treatment plants for the potable water, waste water and industrial water.

With our innovative, technically and socio-economically adapted water treatment solutions, we contribute to the global effort to keep water resources clean and provide people around the world with access to clean, safe water.

Industrial waste water

Since the values of pollutants and characteristics of the processability of industrial wastewater vary, there is a need to develop special solutions for use in wastewater treatment plants.

Large-Panel House Building Plant

During the construction of residential buildings, it is necessary to equip construction objects with related products, which are critical for the construction completion. External wall panels serve to enclose the premises from the external space, and take up loads from their own weight, wind loads and loads of floors and roofs.

Oil refinery plant

The main activity of the refinery is oil refining and production of high-quality fuel.
Main types of products are:

  • gasoline and Euro-5 diesel fuel
  • aviation kerosene, marine fuel
  • polymers for the production of PVC windows, pipes, packaging
  • bitumen for road works
  • granular sulfur for fertilizer production
Local treatment facilities

The local treatment facilities include large-scale complexes, consisting of several large-format structures. Treatment systems are used to process effluent from industrial enterprises, household facilities, private houses. Structures are built outside cities and towns. The territories allocated for the functioning of such complexes are unsuitable for living and recreation. The facilities are serviced by specially trained employees. The control is carried out using automatic equipment.

Biological Treatment Structures

Biological treatment structures are tanks or constructions used to treat wastewater using bacteria. The same as aerotanks, they are included in the centralized drainage system or local treatment complex. In addition to bacteria, biofilters contain materials that trap out coarse dirt.
